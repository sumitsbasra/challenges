Privacy Policy

Challenges  ·  Last updated March 2026

Challenges is a fitness app that uses your health data to power competitive challenges with friends. Your health data never leaves Apple's infrastructure.

Data We Collect

Health & Activity Data
With your permission, Challenges reads activity data from Apple HealthKit — including active energy burned, exercise minutes, stand hours, and step count. This data is used solely to calculate your challenge scores and display your activity rings. It is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than running your challenges.

Account Information
Your display name and profile photo are stored in your iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. This data is associated with your Apple ID and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Challenge Data
Challenge details, invite codes, participation records, and daily scores are stored in Apple CloudKit. This data is only visible to participants in challenges you've joined.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not have access to your HealthKit data on our servers — it stays on your device and in your iCloud account

Apple HealthKit

HealthKit data is used only to calculate fitness scores within the app. It will not be used for advertising, will not be shared with third parties, and will not be used for any purpose beyond providing core app functionality.

Data Retention

Your data persists as long as your iCloud account is active. Deleting the app removes locally cached data. To delete your CloudKit data, sign out of the app and contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Challenges is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
